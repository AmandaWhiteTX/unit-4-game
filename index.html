Crystal Collector!

At the start of the game you will be given a random number to match. Each of the crystals below will add a specific amount of points to your total score. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. Each time when the game starts, the values of each crystal is reset to a new number of points.
